Our Story

Welcome to the HerInTech Magazine — where curiosity meets innovation. Our magazine is dedicated to capturing the spirit, creativity, and achievements of young girls who are exploring the exciting world of technology through this HerInTech Summer Camp.

Founded by a team of passionate creatives and problem-solvers, HerInTech Magazine began as a small idea scribbled in a notebook. Today, it's a growing platform where designers, developers, and dreamers come together to share knowledge, spark conversations, and bring bold ideas to life.

The HerInTech Camp is more than just a camp — it's a journey of discovery. Here, young girls learn while having fun essential skills in coding and design, guided by mentors and facilitators who believe in their potential to shape the future. We believe tech is not just a tool, but a language — one that empowers us to turn ideas into reality.

We are passionate about fostering a culture of learning, innovation and inclusiveness — because the future of technology belongs to us. Whether you're here to stay informed, get inspired, or share your voice, HerInTech Magazine is your platform to explore the ever-evolving world of tech.

We stand by these main values:

  • Creativity: We strive to think differently
  • Collaboration: We grow better together
  • Innovation: We embrace new ideas
  • Impact: We design and code with purpose and impact on real experiences
Whether you're here to read our magazine, join our workshops, or simply explore, know this: you're part of a community that believes in making tech human again.

So grab your drink, take a look around, and let's build something meaningful together.

Our Mission

Founded to empower girls in Rwanda and beyond, HerInTech is a platform that celebrates women in technology. Our story began with a vision to create accessible tech education and mentorship for young girls.

We organize camps, workshops, and online resources that allow girls to explore technology, build confidence, and discover career paths they never thought possible.

Our mission extends beyond just teaching technical skills. We aim to:

Our Core Objectives:

  • Inspire: Show girls that technology is accessible and exciting
  • Educate: Provide quality tech education and resources
  • Empower: Build confidence and leadership skills
  • Connect: Create a supportive community of women in tech
  • Impact: Drive positive change in our local and global communities

Through our magazine, camps, and community programs, we're building a generation of confident, skilled, and inspired women who will lead the future of technology in Rwanda and across Africa.
