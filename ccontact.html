Frequently Asked Questions

How can I join the HerInTech camp?

Our camps are open to girls and young women interested in technology. Applications typically open 2-3 months before each camp. Follow us on social media or subscribe to our newsletter for announcements.

Can I volunteer as a mentor or facilitator?

Absolutely! We're always looking for passionate tech professionals to mentor our participants. Contact us with your background and availability, and we'll connect you with opportunities that match your expertise.

How can I contribute to the magazine?

We welcome stories, articles, and insights from women in tech. Whether you want to share your journey, technical knowledge, or career advice, reach out to us with your ideas.

Do you offer corporate partnerships?

Yes! We partner with companies that share our vision of empowering women in technology. Partnerships can include sponsorships, internship programs, mentorship opportunities, and more.

Is there a cost to participate in HerInTech programs?

We strive to keep our programs accessible. Many of our workshops and camps are free or low-cost, supported by donations and sponsorships. Scholarship opportunities are also available.
